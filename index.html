Our Mission

We exist to ensure that every fried burrito meets the highest standards of taste, texture, and safety. Through rigorous inspection processes and an unwavering commitment to quality, we safeguard consumers from the threat of subpar burritos, guaranteeing that no crispy tortilla or savory filling is left unchecked.

Our Services

Exhaustive Inspections: Every burrito we evaluate undergoes an intensive inspection, from the crispness of the fry to the balance of ingredients. Only those that meet our exacting standards earn the FBI certification.

Certification Process: Burrito vendors who pass our strict evaluation are awarded the FBI seal of approval. This prestigious certification ensures consumers that they are experiencing the very best fried burritos available.

Consumer Protection: We are here to empower you, the consumer, by providing the tools and knowledge necessary to identify and avoid inferior burritos. When it comes to fried burritos, you deserve only the finest.

Why Trust the FBI?

Expert Inspectors: Our team consists of seasoned food safety experts, culinary specialists, and professional burrito inspectors—all dedicated to maintaining the highest standards in fried burrito excellence.

Uncompromising Standards: We do not take shortcuts. If a burrito doesn’t meet our exacting criteria, we take action to protect your taste buds and ensure a burrito that truly lives up to its potential.

A Focus on Quality: Our commitment to quality is unwavering. By joining our cause, you are supporting a community that is striving for the ultimate in fried burrito perfection.
